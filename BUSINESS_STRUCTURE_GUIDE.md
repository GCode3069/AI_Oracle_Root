## üè¢ RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE

### **Tier 1: Core Entities (Week 1)**

#### **1. LLC (Delaware/Wyoming) - Main Operating Entity**
```
Entity: "SCARIFY MEDIA LLC" (or similar)
State: Delaware (corporate-friendly) OR Wyoming (privacy-friendly)
Purpose: Main content creation, YouTube channel ownership
Benefits:
  - Pass-through taxation (no double tax)
  - Asset protection
  - Easy to set up ($100-300)
  - Can convert to C-corp later if needed
```

**Setup Steps:**
1. Choose name (check availability)
2. File Articles of Organization ($50-200)
3. Get EIN from IRS (free)
4. Open business bank account
5. Operating Agreement (template or lawyer)

**Cost:** $200-500 initial
**Time:** 1-2 weeks

---

#### **2. Separate Entity for Affiliate/Product Sales**

**Setup:** Same as above
