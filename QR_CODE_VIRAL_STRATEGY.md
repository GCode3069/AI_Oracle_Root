## üì± PLATFORM-SPECIFIC QR STRATEGIES

- **Duration:** Show QR for 5-8 seconds
- **Placement:** Center screen, full size
- **Hook:** First 0.5s before code
- **Audio:** "Scan this NOW" voiceover
- **Duet:** Enable for reaction videos
- **Stitch:** Let others build on it

### **Instagram Reels:**
- **Duration:** Show QR for 6-10 seconds
- **Placement:** Lower third initially, then full screen
- **Aesthetic:** Match brand colors
- **Story:** Post QR in Stories with swipe-up alternative
- **Save prompt:** "Save and scan later"

### **YouTube Shorts:**
- **Duration:** QR visible entire video (60s)
- **Placement:** Corner (persistent) + full screen (5s)
- **Description:** Backup link in description
- **Pinned comment:** "Can't scan? Click here"

### **Twitter/X:**
- **Format:** Static image + video
- **Reply:** QR code in reply thread
- **Quote tweet:** Make it easy to share
- **Alt text:** Backup link for accessibility

### **Reddit:**
- **Subreddit:** Target specific communities
- **Format:** High-res image
- **Comment:** Context in top comment
- **Cross-post:** Multiple relevant subs
