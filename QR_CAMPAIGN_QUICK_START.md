## üìà OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### **Increase Scan Rates:**

1. **Make QR Larger** - 60%+ of screen
2. **Show Longer** - 8-10 seconds minimum
3. **Add Movement** - Pulse/glitch animation
4. **Use Color** - High contrast
5. **Verbal CTA** - "SCAN THIS NOW"
6. **Add Urgency** - Timer/countdown
7. **Social Proof** - "X people scanned"

### **Platform-Specific:**

**TikTok:**
- Show QR at 3s mark (after hook)
- Keep visible until 10s
- Use trending sound
- Enable duets/stitches

**Instagram:**
- Full-screen QR at 5s
- Story with swipe-up backup
- "Save and scan later" prompt
- Aesthetic integration

**YouTube Shorts:**
- QR in corner entire video
- Full-screen at end
- Link in description
- Pinned comment backup

**Twitter:**
- Static image + video
- QR in first reply
- Quote tweet friendly
- Alt text for accessibility
